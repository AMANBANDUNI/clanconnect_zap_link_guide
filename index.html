How to use Zap

Zap turns a single Instagram comment into an automatic DM. When a follower comments a keyword on your Post, Reel, or Story, ClanConnect instantly replies to the comment and slides into their DMs — with your message or a rich Zap Link product card.

Before you begin

Connect Instagram & allow message access

Zap needs a connected Instagram Business/Creator account and one Instagram-side permission. If it’s missing, the app shows the “Important Instagram Setting” sheet.

Turn on “Allow access to Messages”

Inside the Instagram app:

  1. Open Instagram → Menu.
  2. Settings and activity → Messages and story replies.
  3. Message Controls → Connected Tools.
  4. Toggle ON “Allow access to Messages”.
  5. Back in ClanConnect, tap It’s Done!
⚠️
No connected account yet? Zap first asks you to Connect Instagram and grant the basic, comments & messages permissions before this step appears.

Step 1 of 2

Select Posts or Stories

Choose which content the automation watches. Tick one or more items, then tap Next.

Step 2 of 2 · Create New Zap

Name it & configure rules

Give the Zap an Automation Name, then add at least one rule. Create Automation stays greyed out until a name and an active rule exist.

Either way the rules list starts empty and Create Automation is greyed out. Tap + Add Rule to begin.

Anatomy of a rule

Tap + Add Rule to open the New Rule form. A rule is made of:

  • Trigger Keywords — words that fire the automation (added one-by-one with the + icon).
  • Comment Replies — optional public replies posted under the comment.
  • A DM payload — either a Direct Message or a Zap Link.
🔁
Comment reply or message can be optional — but not both empty. One of them must carry content.

① Trigger keywords & comment replies

  1. Type a keyword like INFO, LINK, URL and tap +. Each becomes a chip.
  2. Add a Comment Reply (e.g. “Please check your dm”) and tap +. Add several to rotate randomly.
  3. Pick the DM type below: Direct Message or ⚡ Zap Link.
⚠️
Keyword count, comment replies and rules per Zap are all capped by your plan — you’ll see an Upgrade prompt when you hit a limit.

② Option A — Direct Message

A plain private message

On the Direct Message tab, write the private text sent to the commenter. It can include text, emojis and any clickable link (Google Drive, a product page, docs, etc.).

⚡ The star feature

Option B — Zap Link product cards

Instead of plain text, Zap Link sends rich product cards. Paste a product URL and ClanConnect auto-fetches the image, title, brand, price and discount.

How to add a product

  1. In the rule form, switch to the ⚡ Zap Link tab.
  2. Paste a product URL into https://brand.com/product/… and tap +.
  3. The card auto-populates — e.g. SanDisk Extreme Pro Portable SSD, 12% OFF INR 23919.
  4. You can manually tweak name, brand, price & discount text.
  5. Add up to 10 products per rule (plan-dependent). At least one is required.
  6. Tap Add Rule.
🔒
Zap Link can be gated by plan. On unsupported plans the tab shows an “not available on your current plan” message with an upgrade link.

Go live

Create the automation & manage it

With a name and at least one active rule, Create Automation turns solid — tap it to publish. You’ll land on My Zaps.

On the My Zaps screen you can

  • Toggle a Zap on/off without deleting it.
  • Edit (pencil) to reopen the wizard.
  • Delete (trash) a Zap you no longer need.
  • Filter with “Show active Zaps only”.
  • See keywords, source (Posts/Stories), rule count & trigger count.
  • Open Interactions to view what got triggered.
✅
That’s it — you’re live. The next time someone comments your keyword, they’ll get an instant comment reply and a DM (text or Zap Link product card), fully automatically.
